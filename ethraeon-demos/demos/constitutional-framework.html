Constitutional AI Framework

A practical way to ship AI safely: policies → checks → actions → audit.

What ETHRAEON is

ETHRAEON is a governance-first layer that sits between your models and your products. It gives teams a common set of policies, checks, and audit trails so AI features can be deployed with confidence in regulated and brand-sensitive environments.

The core loop

  1. Declare policies (e.g., “no protected-class stereotyping”, “cite sources for medical claims”).
  2. Instrument checks that evaluate model inputs/outputs against those policies.
  3. Decide actions based on risk (allow, redact, request human review, block, escalate).
  4. Log & audit every decision with reasons, so you can explain what happened later.

How the demos map to the framework

  • FactPulse — applies checks for misinformation signals and recommends next actions.
  • Constitutional Bias Dashboard — shows policies, pass/fail, and what would block a release.
  • Field Triage — routes risky cases (self-harm, data leaks) with governance-safe playbooks.
  • EcoTrack Pro — demonstrates governance for impact reporting (clear metrics, deltas, auditability).

What “simulated” means here

These pages use realistic sample data so you can click through the workflows instantly. They’re wired exactly like a production setup; swapping in your model, policies, or a database is straightforward (see “Make it live” below).

Constitutional AI Framework

Governance-first architecture that turns policies into checks, guardrails, and audits you can deploy.

Why it exists

  • Enterprises want AI value, but need predictable behavior, risk control, and auditability.
  • Policies live in documents; production systems need executable checks and evidence trails.

The Core Loop

  1. Inputs: prompts, data, models, and user context.
  2. Policies → Checks: translate policy into testable constraints (safety, bias, privacy, brand).
  3. Runtime Controls: refuse/repair/route with human-in-the-loop.
  4. Telemetry & Audit: structured logs, system cards, exportable artifacts.
  5. Review & Improve: close the loop with drift monitoring and retraining gates.

Where the demos fit

  • FactPulse Pro → Runtime signals + actions.
  • Bias Dashboard → Policy checks & audit bundles.
  • Field Triage → Intake → classify → governed actions.
  • EcoTrack Pro → Outcomes tracking with operational playbooks.

Deployment model

  • Start: instrument one workflow; enable checks and exportable audits.
  • Scale: add policies as code; route events to your ticketing/ChatOps.
  • Assure: generate system cards, bias reports, and compliance exports on schedule.

Ethraeon — Constitutional AI Framework

Practical governance you can ship. Start with one workflow, bake in guardrails, and export audits on demand.

Core loop

  • Observe: instrument a decision (post, case, transaction).
  • Check: run policy tests (bias, risk, safety, compliance).
  • Act: route to humans/systems (ticket, escalation, notify).
  • Assure: log outcomes and export system cards/audits.

What the demos show

  • FactPulse: risk signals + suggested actions (with sources).
  • Field Triage: classify, score severity, recommend next steps.
  • Bias Dashboard: pass/fail checks with exportable audit bundle.
  • EcoTrack: deltas → one-click operational playbook.

Deployment model

  • Start: pick a single workflow and light up checks + exports.
  • Scale: codify more policies; wire actions to your tools (Jira/ServiceNow/Slack/Email).
  • Assure: schedule bias reports & system cards; keep auditors and leadership in the loop.

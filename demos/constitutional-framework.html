Ethraeon — Constitutional AI Framework

This framework brings evaluation, safeguards, and transparency into live systems. It is designed to scale across domains — from content integrity to compliance, operations, and financial governance.

Core Loop

  • Ingest — capture inputs (content, actions, data streams)
  • Check — evaluate against guardrails, rules, and policies
  • Act — apply outcomes: alerts, blocks, reports, or escalations

Deployment Path

  • Start small: run one demo with sample data
  • Scale: connect to live systems (Slack, Jira, CRM)
  • Assure: export reports for audits & compliance
  • Start: Run any demo with sample data to see the framework in action.
  • Scale: Connect to live systems such as Slack, Jira, CRM, or internal dashboards.
  • Assure: Export transparent audit and compliance reports at the press of a button.

Why It Matters

  • Trust at speed — guardrails that hold even in fast-moving systems.
  • Transparency — evaluations and risks made visible, not hidden.
  • Scalability — the same loop applies across any enterprise function.
